#### Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created to help people who struggle with focus and building routines, especially neurodivergent users. Many productivity apps do not fully address their needs, while Focus Bear is specifically designed to support people with ADHD and similar challenges.

#### What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Helping people schedule routines and focus on their routine, blocking distractions from their mobile phone or computer.

#### Why do you think this mission is important?
This mission is important because the app is centered on neurodivergent users and is developed by people who understand these challenges firsthand. This allows Focus Bear to better address real user needs and provide meaningful support that can significantly improve users’ quality of life.

#### How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?
I have always enjoyed creating applications that help or simplify tasks for others. Working on a productivity app designed to support and help people aligns strongly with my interest in building useful and impactful software.

#### Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
Yes. I often get distracted by apps like YouTube or TikTok while studying. Being able to block these distractions helps me refocus on what is important and manage my time better.
