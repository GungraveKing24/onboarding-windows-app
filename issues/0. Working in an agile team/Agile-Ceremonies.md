#### What are the main Agile ceremonies, and what is their purpose?
* Daily Stand-ups: Brief meetings where team members share their work, plans, and obstacles

* Sprint Planning (Scrum) vs. Continuous Prioritization (Kanban): 
* (Scrum) The team plans a fixed set of work for sprints; goals and tasks are defined in advance 
* (Kanban) Prioritizes continuous work without sprints, allowing for flexibility and quick adjustments
* Retrospectives: Periodic meetings to reflect on what went well and what didn't, and how to improve processes and collaboration.
* Backlog Refinement: Sessions where upcoming tasks are clarified, estimated, and prioritized.

#### How do Agile teams collaborate asynchronously and across time zones?
Agile teams use written updates, task boards, feedback, documentation, and recorded meetings. Shared resources help maintain transparency without requiring everyone to be online simultaneously.
