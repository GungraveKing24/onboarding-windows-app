### What is Agile, and how does it differ from traditional project management?
Is an iterative and flexible approach to software development that focuses on delivering small, continuous improvements. Agil adapts to change, encourages frequent feedback.

### What are the core values and principles of Agile?
Agile principles emphasize continuous delivery, welcoming people

### What are the differences between Scrum and Kanban, and when is each used?
Scrum has fixed sprint durations, defined roles, and regular meetings. Kanban is a continuous flow system without fixed sprints or roles; it focuses on visualizing work, limiting work in progress, and improving flow.

### Why does Focus Bear lean towards Kanban instead of Scrum?
Because it allows for flexibility in work and continuous delivery.
