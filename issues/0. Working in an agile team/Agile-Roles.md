#### What are the key roles in an Agile team, and what does each role do? 

* **Developers**: Design and build features. They write code, fix bugs, and ensure the application works efficiently and reliably.
* **QA (Quality Assurance)**: Test the product to identify bugs and verify that features meet quality standards before release.
* **Product Manager (PM)**: Defines the product vision, prioritizes features, and ensures the work aligns with business and user needs.
* **Agile Project Manager / Scrum Master**: Helps remove blockers, organizes ceremonies, and supports the team in staying on track.
* **Stakeholders**: Provide feedback, requirements, and business goals to guide product direction.
