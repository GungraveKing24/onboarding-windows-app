#### What are the key roles in an Agile team, and what does each role do? 
* Developers: Design and build. They write code and fix bugs, ensure the application work efficiently and reliably
* QA (Quality Assurance): Test the product to identify bugs and ensure features meet quality standars.
* Product Manager (PM): Define the product vision and prioritis.
* Agile Project Manager (Agile PM): The remove blockers, organize ceremonies and hel the team sty on track.
* Stakeholders: Provide input, feedback and business goals. Ensure the product aligns with user needs.
